Listen

To people-generated content

Explore the city a new way through content tailored for seemingly no pattern. It's an experience sharing platform. You will be hearing from someone speaking from the exact location you're standing on.

Speak

Populate the area with your own content

We're trying to make it as easy as possible to share your own experience. If you have obscure knowledge about your location, hit record and your voice will now be linked to your exact location. Anyone who walks by this spot now will here your voice. For all eternity. Or at least until we run out of server money.

Yes, this is risky business

Eventually we hope to build in a voting system so it doesn't get too spammy. And the best files will (ideally) be voted to the top and bad actors get removed.
We will also be building free audio tours ourselves to this platform and currate all the tours we can to populate this thingy!
